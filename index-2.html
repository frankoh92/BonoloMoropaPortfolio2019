About Me

Hey There, Im Bonolo, A midweight-designer based in Diepkloof, Soweto

Graphic Content | Web Designer

My core strength is paying attention to detail and I pride myself in maintaining the good reputation I have on completing tasks perfectly and meeting deadlines.
